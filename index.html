Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for your safety and compliance needs

A

Audits & Surveys

We identify shortcomings within your occupancy through various specialized audits including hazardous chemical substances, occupational hygiene, general health and safety, fire investigation and prevention, and more.

L

Legal Compliance

Our Legal Compliance Audits determine your compliance with applicable legislation (SANS, NBR, OHSA, and CIDA) and Bylaws, ensuring your business meets all regulatory requirements.

I

ISO Implementation

We assist with development and introduction of international systems and standards including ISO 14001, ISO 45001, and ISO 9001, offering short to long term on-site solutions.

R

Risk Assessments

We conduct comprehensive workplace risk assessments covering fire risks, environmental risks, hazardous chemical risks, production areas, administrative offices, and transportation.

I

Incident Investigation

Our team investigates workplace accidents and incidents to identify causes and recommend preventative measures to eliminate recurrences.

D

Disaster Management

We guide clients through drafting and implementing emergency evacuation and disaster management preparedness plans to ensure safety during unforeseen events.
